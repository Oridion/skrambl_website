SKRAMBL is a privacy-first Solana wallet that routes transactions through the Oridion protocol to break linkability.

Watch Demo Read Docs

🚀 How it works

Generate a delivery

Choose amount, destination, and an optional delay. A Pod account is created on-chain using Seed Vault keys.

Scramble via Oridion

SOL travels through randomized planetary hops with delays and splitting funds.

Delivery

Funds are automatically released to the destination. The Pod account closes, leaving no active link to the source.

Privacy features

  • No direct source → destination link
  • Randomized routing across planetary accounts
  • Variable delays disrupt timing analysis
  • Generate Real Burner wallets for high-anonymity payments
  • Seed Vault integration keeps keys on-device
  • Generate over 360 hop over a 24hr delay

Privacy Fee

Flat, predictable, and not percentage-based. Unlike mixers that take a cut, SKRAMBL pricing is lamport-fixed—thanks to Solana’s low costs and Oridion’s design.

Example: 24-hour route (over 450 hops)

7,000,000 + (~480 hops × 50,000) = 31,000,000 lamports

That’s 0.031 SOL total for ~24h privacy routing.

💡 Why this beats mixers

Mixers typically charge a % of your transfer. SKRAMBL’s fee is a flat lamport amount — so large transfers aren’t penalized.

This is the cost whether you are sending 0.1 SOL or 100 SOL.
